Red Branch Media (RBM) was founded in 2008 and offers a holistic marketing approach for any business. RBM has served clients of all sizes across numerous industries, including financial, design, software, and technology. Until late 2011, RBM was used mostly in-between "day jobs" and as a place to do consulting as needed. Today, it has grown into a niche agency devoted to B2B marketing for the workplace, mainly the Technology, Nonprofit, Human Resources, and Recruiting spaces.
